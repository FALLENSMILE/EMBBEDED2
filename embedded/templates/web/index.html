About Embedded Systems

Embedded systems are computer systems with a dedicated function within a larger mechanical or electrical system. They are essential in modern technology, ranging from consumer electronics to industrial applications. In this course, we explore various activities and projects to understand how embedded systems work in real-world scenarios.
